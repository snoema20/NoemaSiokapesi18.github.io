San Antonio

The San Antonio Texas Temple is the 120th operating temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. On June 24, 2001 the LDS Church announced it would build a temple in San Antonio, Texas, making it the state's fourth. Within ten years, the number of church members in the area grew from 9,000 to 12,000.
