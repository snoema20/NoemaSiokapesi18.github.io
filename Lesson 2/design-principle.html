White Space and Clean Design

Cosco

Cosco.com

White Space increasing the number of choices increases the decision time on a website. Cosco has a minimal number of choices on their home page, making it easier to decide what one wants to do on their page.

Visual Hierarchy

Apple

Coscor

Cosco uses white space, the area between the elements on their pages, effectively, giving the site a clean, simple look. This example also uses Hick's Law effectively giving the user just enough choice to keep looking, without being overwhelmed.

PARC: Contrast

Target

Target.com

Target uses contrast effectively on their website. The use of red on a white background creates visual interest and visual hierarchy directing the user to the important parts of the page. They have also used white space and Hick's Law effectively.
