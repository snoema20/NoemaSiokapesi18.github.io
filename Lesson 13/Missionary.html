Meet the Missionaries!

We are a proud chain of hotels that loves to help those who are serving the Lord. Often times it can be difficult to find housing for missionaries, so we take care of that! We work closely with mission presidents as we house their missionaries and make sure that they are well taken care of. For those who do not know, The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saint's missionaries consist of young men, women, and couples who choose to spend their time away from their homes and families to teach the restored gospel of Jesus Christ. You may have seen a duo of young men or women walking down the streets with a black name tag attached to their shirts, or a husband and wife with those same name tags in your town, and normally with a smile on their faces. Those are the missionaries!

If you are interested in finding the missionaries assigned to the area in which you are visiting, go visit the Temple page on this website and contact the temple. They will give you the information to be able to reach out to the missionaries who are serving in the area. If you are in need of help around your home or need some extra blessings in your life, make sure to reach out to these faithful servants, because they are always looking for ways to serve!

For missionaries who are staying with us, we offer many suites that provide all that they need. These suites come with:

-2 Queen-sized Beds
-2 Desks
-Bathroom (Shower, Bathtub, Toilet, and 2 Sinks)
-Kitchen (Fridge, Stove, Microwave, Sink and Dishwasher)
-Living room area for visitors

If you'd like to learn more, just fill out our Contact Form below and we will answer any questions that you may have. We look forward to meeting you!
