The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. The owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company.

Meridian, Idaho Temple: this temple was first announced in the April 2011 general conference by President Thomas S. Monson of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The announcement of the Meridian temple marked the 5th temple to be built in the state of Idaho. It took three years for the location of the temple to be chosen, and the site, which is on Linder road that leads to Eagle Island State Park, was chosen because the temple is set on a hill, "which cannot be hid." After ground broke, the construction of the temple began. During the construction of the temple, a group of people broke onto the site and vandalized it, prolonging the time it took to finish. For more information on this event, you can visit this website to learn more. In the Fall of 2017, the temple was opened for tours and was later dedicated.
